📚 Research Overview

My research journey spans distributed systems, networking protocols, and business process management. I've contributed to academic publications and applied research in industry settings, focusing on innovative solutions in content-centric networking and Internet of Things.

🔬 Research Experience

Publish/Subscribe Support in Content-Centric Networking

May 2014 - December 2014

Middleware Systems Research Group, University of Toronto

Investigated PADRES (Publish/Subscribe Applied to Distributed Resource Scheduling) and extended networking capabilities by adding Content-Centric Networking (CCN) as a third communication protocol, complementing the existing TCP and RMI protocols. Optimized the entire system by developing CCN-PS and conducted performance testing on various platforms from local Linux servers to the SciNet HPC cluster. This research resulted in a publication on publish/subscribe support in content-centric networking.

Internet of Things (IoT) Research & Development

MakeWorks

Subject-Oriented Business Process Management

May 2012 - September 2012

FH Joanneum

Led research on Subject-Oriented Business Process Management (S-BPM), modeling and executing operational processes and workflows using jBPM. Research focused on optimizing efficiency by implementing external features in existing S-BPM Software using Java and network programming, including the ability to access external web services and interact with Microsoft Dynamics NAV ERP system. Created an external web service using Java SDK 7 and Apache Tomcat development environment to be used by S-BPM software.

About this garden

Colophon Last tended 5 Jul 2026

This is a digital garden, not a portfolio. A portfolio is finished and sells you something; a garden is tended, changes over time, and is happy to show half-grown ideas. I write here to think in public about how financial systems actually work.

What grows here

Three threads, all kept conceptual and general:

  • Payments under the hood — the mechanics of moving money.
  • The geopolitics of money — who controls the rails, and why that's power.
  • AI as leverage — where machine intelligence really changes the economics of finance.

How to read the labels

  • Stub a placeholder — a title and a sentence, waiting to be written.
  • 🌱 Seedling young and growing — real but unfinished, likely to change.
  • Evergreen tended enough that I trust it, though nothing here is ever truly "done".

How to get around

Writing holds long-form essays grouped into series; Notes is the flat, atomic space; and everything is cross-linked by tag so you can follow a thread by interest. The changelog records what's been tended lately.

How it's built

Deliberately boring: hand-written HTML and CSS, system fonts only, a sprinkle of JavaScript for the light/dark toggle, and inline SVG for the hand-drawn diagrams. Fast to load, easy to tend.

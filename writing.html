Essays, in Seasons

The essays are grouped into Seasons — series with a deliberate reading order. Each piece stands alone, but read top to bottom a Season builds an argument. Everything is explainer-first: I define the jargon, then take a position.

These essays are canonical here — this is the source of record. I syndicate them to Substack, but the version on this page is the one I keep tending.
